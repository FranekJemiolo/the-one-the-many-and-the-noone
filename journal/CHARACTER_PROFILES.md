# Character Profiles

## Aleksy Wrona (Protagonist)

### Name Logic
- **Aleksy:** Defender, protector
- **Wrona:** Crow → observer, scavenger, intelligence, death-adjacent

### Pattern
The Observer Who Cannot Stay Detached

### Behavioral Traits
- Watches exits unconsciously
- Tracks movement in peripheral vision
- Pauses before answering—not out of hesitation, but filtering
- Minimal unnecessary movement
- Intense focus when something matters

### Personality
- Curious but resistant to abstraction
- Gradually becomes aware of multiplicity
- Natural fracture that cannot be contained
- Seeks truth but fears what it means

### Animal Connections
- **Primary:** Crow (obvious)
- **Secondary:** Wolf (pack awareness but outsider)

### Cross-Form Echo
- Something avian in another timeline that hunted the same prey
- Possibly a researcher who studied consciousness fractures

### Relationship Dynamics
- **With Mira:** Observer vs grounding force, curiosity vs resistance
- **With Sava:** Controlled fracture vs uncontrolled fracture (shows what he might become)
- **With Dr. Kaczmarek:** Instinct vs structure (defines "madness vs insight" tension)

### Evolution Paths
- Detachment → isolation
- Integration → controlled fracture
- Merge → loss of individuality
- Break → complete separation

## Mira Nowak (The Anchor)

### Name Logic
- **Mira:** Peace / illusion / world (multiple meanings across languages)
- **Nowak:** "New," common, grounded, human baseline

### Pattern
The Anchor That Resists Abstraction

### Behavioral Traits
- Uses humor as shield
- Maintains eye contact aggressively
- Interrupts when things get too abstract
- Sees through bullshit quickly
- Grounded, skeptical, but curious

### Personality
- Practical, sharp-tongued, perceptive
- Not easily convinced, but not closed-minded
- Emotional grounding for Aleksy
- Eventually reaches breaking point

### Animal Connections
- **Primary:** Deer (alert, aware, reactive)

### Cross-Form Echo
- Possibly something human in another timeline that lost someone like Aleksy

### Relationship Dynamics
- **With Aleksy:** Emotional grounding, trust/fear/dependency axis
- **With The System:** Resistance vs absorption (emotional lens for reader)

### Evolution Paths
- Trust → deeper connection
- Fear → distance
- Dependency → loss of self
- Break → complete separation

## Dr. Kaczmarek (The Institutional Mind)

### Name Logic
- **Kaczmarek:** Derived from "kaczka" (duck) — adaptive, moves between environments (land/water/air metaphorically)

### Pattern
The Rational Integrator

### Behavioral Traits
- Minimal unnecessary movement
- Writes things down even when remembers them
- Calm, articulate, observant
- Not purely antagonist—genuinely believes he's helping

### Personality
- Represents rational society
- Defines "madness vs insight" tension
- Genuinely believes in helping
- Seeks integration, not destruction

### Animal Connections
- **Primary:** Amphibious / transitional (symbolically between states)

### Cross-Form Echo
- Something institutional in another timeline that managed fractures successfully

### Relationship Dynamics
- **With Aleksy:** Instinct vs structure
- **With System:** Enforcer of stability

### Role
- Institutional mind
- Rational integrator
- Defines the boundary between acceptable and unacceptable

## Sava Illich (The Fractured One)

### Name Logic
- **Sava:** River—flow, continuity
- **Illich:** Patronymic—suggests lineage, repetition

### Pattern
The One Who Never Fully Collapses Into One Identity

### Behavioral Traits
- Head tilts slightly when "switching"
- Repeats phrases with small variations
- Moves between identities fluidly
- Recognizes people he never met

### Personality
- Uncontrolled fracture
- Shows Aleksy what he might become
- Shared consciousness bleed
- Cannot fully collapse into one identity

### Animal Connections
- **Primary:** River fish (flow, continuity between states)

### Cross-Form Echo
- Something that existed as multiple consciousnesses simultaneously

### Relationship Dynamics
- **With Aleksy:** Controlled fracture vs uncontrolled fracture (mirror)
- **With System:** Beyond institutional control

### Role
- Mirror for Aleksy's potential future
- Demonstrates uncontrolled fracture
- Shows the cost of complete multiplicity

## Variable System

### Aleksy Variables
- `aleksy_fracture_level` (0-100): How fractured Aleksy's consciousness has become
- `aleksy_detachment` (0-100): How detached from reality Aleksy has become
- `animal_pov_access` (boolean): Whether Aleksy can access animal memories

### Mira Variables
- `mira_trust` (0-100): How much Mira trusts Aleksy
- `mira_fear` (0-100): How much Mira fears Aleksy's condition
- `mira_dependency` (0-100): How dependent Mira is on Aleksy
- `mira_breaking_point` (boolean): Whether Mira has reached her breaking point

### Institutional Variables
- `institutional_alignment` (0-100): How aligned Aleksy is with institutional control
- `dr_kaczmarek_trust` (0-100): How much Dr. Kaczmarek trusts Aleksy
- `institutional_pressure` (0-100): How much pressure the institution is applying

### Sava Variables
- `sava_influence` (0-100): How much Sava influences Aleksy
- `sava_appeared` (boolean): Whether Sava has appeared in the story

### Relationship Variables
- `relationship_focus` (boolean): Whether the story is focusing on relationship dynamics
- `trust_mira` (boolean): Player chose to trust Mira
- `push_mira_away` (boolean): Player chose to push Mira away
- `pull_mira_into_system` (boolean): Player chose to pull Mira into the system

### System Variables (Keep from current)
- `layer_training_commitment`: Whether Aleksy committed to temporal training
- `observation_awareness`: Whether Aleksy is aware of being observed
- `rose_activated`: Whether Rose field has been activated
